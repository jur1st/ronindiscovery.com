In feudal Japan, when samurai became masterless - now a ronin - wandered the land, offering his skills to those who sought truth and justice.

Without allegiance to any master but honor itself, the ronin uncovered secrets others could not see. Where others saw only shadows, the ronin discovered paths through the darkness.

Some say the spirit of the ronin still exists today - in those who seek truth without constraint, who follow evidence where others fear to tread.
